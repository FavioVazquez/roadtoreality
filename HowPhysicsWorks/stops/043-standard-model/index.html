Key Concept
  • The Standard Model catalogs 12 fermions (matter particles) and 5 bosons (force carriers). It explains three of the four fundamental forces with extraordinary precision — but excludes gravity.
→ The Standard Model is our best theory. The Higgs boson completes it — giving mass to all other particles via the Higgs field that permeates space.
