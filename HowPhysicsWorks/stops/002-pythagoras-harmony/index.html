Pythagoras and Mathematical Harmony

Music, numbers, and the shape of the cosmos

~500 BCE · Pythagoras

Pythagoras discovered that musical harmony follows precise mathematical ratios — and concluded that mathematics is the deep structure of reality. When he plucked strings of different lengths, he found that simple integer ratios produced consonant sounds: a 2:1 ratio gives an octave, 3:2 a perfect fifth. This was the first time anyone had linked abstract number to physical phenomenon.

Key Concept
  • Mathematics is not just a tool for describing nature — Pythagoras argued it is the very substance of nature. Every physical law we have discovered since is written in mathematics.
