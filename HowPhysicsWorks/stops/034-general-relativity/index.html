General Relativity: Curved Spacetime

Gravity is the curvature of space and time

1915 · Albert Einstein

Einstein replaced Newton's gravitational force with curved spacetime. Mass tells spacetime how to curve; curved spacetime tells mass how to move.

Key Concept
  • Gravity is not a force — it is the curvature of spacetime caused by mass-energy. Objects follow geodesics (the straightest paths) in curved spacetime, which appear as gravitational attraction.
→ Spacetime curves. But electrons have no definite position in that spacetime — they are waves of probability. De Broglie proposed that matter, like light, has a wavelength.
