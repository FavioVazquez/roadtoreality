Faraday's Electromagnetic Induction

Move a magnet through a coil — electricity appears

1831 · Michael Faraday

Faraday — a bookbinder's apprentice with no formal education — discovered that a changing magnetic field creates an electric current. Every generator on Earth runs on this principle.

Key Concept
  • A changing magnetic flux through a loop induces an EMF. Faraday's law: ε = −dΦ/dt. This is how every electric generator and transformer on Earth operates.
→ Faraday gave us generators. Carnot asked a harder question: how much work can you extract from heat? His answer set a limit that no engineer has ever beaten — and never will.
