Hertz and Radio Waves

Maxwell's invisible light, detected at last

1887 · Heinrich Hertz

Hertz built a spark-gap transmitter and detected the electromagnetic waves Maxwell had predicted — proving that invisible radiation travels at the speed of light.

Key Concept
  • Hertz confirmed Maxwell's prediction experimentally: oscillating charges radiate electromagnetic waves at the speed of light. This discovery made radio, television, and wireless communication possible.
→ Hertz detected Maxwell's waves. Michelson and Morley attempted to measure Earth's motion through the ether — and found nothing. Their null result pointed directly toward Einstein.
