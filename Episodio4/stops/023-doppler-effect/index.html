The Doppler Effect

Why an ambulance sounds different as it passes

1842 · Christian Doppler

Moving sources compress or stretch the waves they emit. Doppler worked this out mathematically — it became the key to measuring the expansion of the universe.

Teaser — full interactive simulation coming in Phase 9

What you'll explore in the full stop

  • → Adjust source velocity and observe how wavefront compression changes frequency

Key Concept
  • A source moving toward you compresses its waves — you hear/see a higher frequency. Moving away stretches them — lower frequency. Δf/f ≈ v/c for slow sources.
→ Doppler connected motion to frequency. Boltzmann connected temperature to microscopic motion — and defined entropy as a count of possible arrangements.
