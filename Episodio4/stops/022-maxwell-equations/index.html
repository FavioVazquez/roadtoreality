Maxwell's Equations

Four equations that contain all of classical electromagnetism

1865 · James Clerk Maxwell

Maxwell unified electricity, magnetism, and light into four elegant equations — and predicted that light itself is an electromagnetic wave.

Key Concept
  • Four equations unify electricity, magnetism, and light. They predict that electromagnetic waves travel at c = 1/√(ε₀μ₀) — exactly the speed of light, proving light is electromagnetic.
→ Maxwell unified electricity and magnetism. Doppler showed that the motion of a source shifts the frequency of the waves it emits — a tool that would later measure the expansion of the universe itself.
