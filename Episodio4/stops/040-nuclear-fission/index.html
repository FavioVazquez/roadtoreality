Key Concept
  • When a heavy nucleus splits, the products weigh slightly less than the original. That missing mass (Δm) becomes energy: ΔE = Δmc². One kilogram of U-235 releases as much energy as 20,000 tonnes of TNT.
→ The nucleus can be split. Feynman, Schwinger, and Tomonaga explained how light and matter interact at the deepest level — quantum electrodynamics, the most precise theory ever written.
